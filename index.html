About Me

Introduction

I'm Bonface Kibuchi, a final-year Bachelor of Pharmacy student at the University of Nairobi, passionate about using technology to transform healthcare. My journey has blended pharmaceutical knowledge with an expanding interest in digital health and software development.

Programming Languages

I’m currently learning and working with Python, along with some HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to build foundational web and data tools.

Educational Background

I'm pursuing a Bachelor of Pharmacy at the University of Nairobi, with hands-on experience from internships at Malibu Pharmacy, Kerugoya County Referral Hospital, and Nerix Pharma Industry. My education is backed by coursework in Pharmacology, Clinical Pharmacy, and Pharmaceutical Chemistry. You can download my CV here .

Interests

I'm driven by a desire to improve health outcomes through technology. My interests include digital health systems, public health innovation, health data literacy, and open-source solutions in healthcare.

Projects

I’ve taken part in tech-health events like the Tech & Health Innovation Week at UoN, and I’m currently exploring how programming can help streamline pharmacy workflows and community outreach efforts. More of my coding projects and ideas will be shared soon.
